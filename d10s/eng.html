The history of [D10S]

Diego Vs Diego on Behance
1
The graphic project begins in the middle of the year 2018, wanting to create a living tribute for Diego Armando Maradona, the graphic is designed in Corel Draw.
2
In mid-October of the same year (2018) I finish my graph, and I title it "Diego Vs Diego", marking a little my vision of Diego, and stating that one of his biggest rivals was himself.
3
October 30, I publish my graphic on Behance "Diego Vs Diego"
4
In the year 2020, I start to make some retouching in some photos, learning a little about the effects of Sketch in Photoshop, this leads me to take up the creation of images in tribute to Diego Armando Maradona.
5
On November 25, 2020, Diego physically leaves us.
6
In the year 2021 I begin to investigate NFTs, what they are and how to create them.
7
The Project takes a new course, on April 14, 2021, the tokens were created on the Bitcoin Cash (SLP) blockchain, taking advantage of cheap transfer rates. After the tokens were approved and verified on GitHub, they were published on the Juungle Market, then time shows that the market stagnates on this platform.
8
In January 2022, the advantages of cheap transfers in Polygon (Matic), and the ease of publication of Opensea, it was decided to publish the same NFTs in the aforementioned market. Waiting for the trend to breathe new life into the D10S NFTs.
